Started reading the Pragmatic Programmer.


Stuff I have learned so far:

  • Duplicate code is bad, dont do it. Use the DRY method
  • Tracer Bullets are your friend - Sniping a project the first time is difficult, instead use tracer bullets and walk it in. Easier to hit the target, and your customers are more involved/feel part of it
  • Domain Languages abstract code, and that can be a good thing
  • Estimating. The most success i have had with estimation is find a task that takes you about 5-6 hours to complete and call that one day (with meetings and ramp up time). Then, use the complexity and moving parts to calculate bigger or smaller. If you hit a number greater than 5 days, 1 sprint, whatever, you really might want to consider breaking it down into more manageable parts.
