Why do I blog/write code?

01/26/2017 16:11:00

I read this quote by Jeff Atwood that has stuck with me, and really made me want to get back into blogging and coding for a difference.

Rather than, ‘What do I want to be when I grow up?’ ask,
‘In what way do I wish the world were different? What problem can I help solve?

One of the ways i want to make the world different is by blogging about things i see, and working on projects to make the world a better place. We all live here on this spinning ball of life, lets work together and continuously make it better!
